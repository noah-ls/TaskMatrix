# Code of Conduct

TaskMatrix aims to be a constructive, practical, and welcoming open-source
project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and professional.
- Assume good intent, but stay clear and concrete.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Accept maintainers' decisions when a topic has been resolved.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or conduct.
- Deliberate disruption of issues, pull requests, or discussions.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainers. If a private contact
address is not listed, open a minimal issue asking for a maintainer contact
without including sensitive details.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block
participants when needed to keep the project healthy.
